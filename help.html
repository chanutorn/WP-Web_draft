How do I subscribe and what are the available subscription plans?
To subscribe to Watchlist Pro, please visit our Plans page
Once on the Plans page, you can explore the different subscription options available to you. After selecting a suitable plan, simply click on the 'Buy Now' button to proceed to the payment method page.
On the payment method page, you'll be able to choose your preferred currency and payment method. After selecting your payment method, a summary of your payment, along with payment details and terms and conditions, will be displayed. Once you've reviewed and agreed to the terms, click the green 'Purchase' button to complete your purchase.

- Enter your payment details as required.
- Before completing the purchase, you'll have the option to activate 'Automatic Renewal'. Simply check the box to enable this feature.
- To disable auto-renewal
Visit our member site: Watchlist Pro Member Site
- Log in using your Watchlist Pro account credentials.
- Navigate to the 'My Account' tab.
- Alongside your plan details, you'll find an option to cancel auto-renewal.
